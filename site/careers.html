Structural Engineer

Interested candidates may submit an application at this link.

ABOUT THE POSITION:

  • Oke Nichols Engineering, Inc. is a structural engineering design and consulting firm that specializes in custom residential projects. Owners Ben Oke, PE and Dylan Nichols, PE both graduated from NC State and have a combined 15+ years of structural engineering experience. As a company, we pride ourselves on offering customized, client-focused approaches to engineering projects and are looking to add a new member to our team. We are currently seeking applicants with a bachelor’s degree in structural engineering, an FE certification, and anywhere from 0-4 years of prior experience. The ideal candidate will demonstrate a strong work ethic, creative problem-solving skills, high attention to detail, and proficiency in interpersonal communication and collaboration.
  •  
  • This position will provide engaging and challenging assignments that develop the skills needed to build a successful career in the field of structural engineering. As an Oke Nichols engineer, you will work with industry-standard structural engineering design and drafting software such as AutoCAD, Revit, and ENERCALC. Oke Nichols Engineering team members are given their own projects and provided with opportunities to learn from and collaborate with colleagues daily, thus cultivating skillset growth and increased autonomy. Our work environment enables every team member to contribute, and the level of support provided is tailored to the needs of each individual. The Oke Nichols Engineering team operates primarily out of our Raleigh office or in the field, but we also provide opportunities to work from home and scheduling flexibility when needed. The starting salary will be commensurate with experience and qualifications. Performance-based bonuses are awarded quarterly, salary raises are awarded annually.
Responsibilities
  • Learn/interpret current NC Residential Building Code
  • Draft to code in AutoCAD software environment
  • Determine tributary areas of residential structures and performing load calculations on wood, steel, structural composite lumber, and concrete on ENERCALC
  • Compile reports based upon on-site observations of new and existing residential structures, footings, poured-in-place concrete walls, retaining walls, etc.
  • Work both independently and in collaborative efforts to meet established deadlines and accomplish weekly/monthly goals
  • Interact with and respond to clients in a professional and timely manner
Required Applicant Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree in structural engineering
  • Successful completion of the following core courses in fulfillment of an undergraduate degree in structural engineering: Solid Mechanics, Statics, Reinforced Concrete Design, & Steel Design (Wood Design is a plus, but not required)
  • Ability to think analytically/creatively to apply lessons learned in school and on the job to future assignments
  • Fundamentals of Engineering (FE) certification
  • Significant prior experience using AutoCAD design program
  • Familiarity with Microsoft office suite, Adobe Acrobat, and cloud-based meeting platforms (Zoom, Microsoft Teams, etc.)
  • Desire to grow and willingness to be coached
  • Exceptional organization and attention to detail
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Ability to work both independently and as part of a team in a communal workspace environment
  • Foundational understanding of the importance of customer service and client relations
  • Reliable personal transportation that enables consistent punctuality
  • Submission of the following documents: transcripts reflecting required coursework/grades, FE certification, and CV/resume outlining qualifications and prior experience (if any) relevant to the position
Required Documents
  • College/University Transcripts
  • Proof of FE Certification
  • CV/Resume
