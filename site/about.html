About

Oke Nichols Engineering, PLLC is a boutique residential structural engineering and consulting firm specializing in the structural design of traditional, contemporary, and modern homes as well as remodels, additions, and the renovation of existing homes in and around the greater Raleigh area. We choose to spend our time working hand in hand with homeowners, builders, architects, and designers in order to make the home building and renovation process a point of pride and enjoyment for all those involved. Our client centered approach builds not only homes, but lasting relationships as well. Putting customer service first and quality over quantity has enabled us to take the time to get to know you as our clients and to understand your needs as a person and / or company. Whether you are an architect, designer, builder or individual homeowner, we invite you to join our family and to let us be a part of your project, dream home, or renovation today.
