About

Oke Nichols Engineering, Inc. is a structural engineering and consulting firm that specializes in custom residential project design. We have a wide range of experience engineering structural plans for new custom home builds of all sizes and styles as well as remodels, additions, and renovations across NC and surrounding states. We offer customized, client-focused approaches to every design with an emphasis on producing quality work and building lasting client relationships. Our skilled team works with homeowners, builders, architects, and designers to make sure each client’s project is uniquely crafted to meet their individual needs and style. Owners Ben Oke, PE and Dylan Nichols, PE have a combined 15+ years of structural engineering experience and work with the Oke Nichols Engineering team to make every client’s dream home a reality.

Contact Oke Nichols Engineering, Inc. today and let us help you bring your next home project to life.
