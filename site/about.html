About

“I learned the value of hard work by working hard” -Margaret Mead

At Oke Engineering, pllc, we find our meaning in life by helping you solve your “problems”.

No, I’m nowhere near qualified enough to build you a rocket and shoot you into outer space. But, we can find you a way to remove that load bearing wall between your kitchen and dining room. We can find you a way to bump out your master bedroom so you can get that walk-in closet your clothes so desperately deserve. We can find a way for you to build the house of your dreams or remodel your existing one into your new and improved home. We can find a way to solve almost any residential structural, “problem,” and most importantly, we can find a way together.

Being paid to fit a square peg into a round hole is what engineers do.
